Frontline consists of multiple plugins and packages.
Here you get the stripped down vanilla version where you can combine the building blocks to create your own setup.

It is recommend to use the [frontline-starter-spa](/docs/getting-started/flavours/spa) as a baseline, because it consist of our most recent best practices.
If it does not exactly fit your requirements you can just clean up and delete those parts.
But if you want to understand how it is composed and you want to do it yourself - just keep going.
